Key Inflection Points
Events that reshaped adoption curves
2019
Cloud AI platforms mature. Adoption reaches 58%; dominated by IT & finance.
2020
COVID-19 disrupts deployment cycles. Adoption dips to 50%; remote automation accelerates.
2022
MLOps maturity. Firms move from PoC to production. Stable 56% adoption base.
2023 ★
GenAI explosion. ChatGPT, GPT-4, Claude. Adoption surges to 72% in 12 months.
2025
AI agents & agentic workflows. 78% adoption. ROI realization at scale.
