01 · The Surge

One year changed
everything.

Between 2022 and 2023, enterprise AI adoption jumped +17 percentage points — the steepest single-year surge since tracking began. Generative AI compressed a decade of progress into twelve months.

04 · The Bottleneck

The technology is ready.
People aren't.

In 2022, data quality was the #1 barrier to AI adoption. By 2024, the talent gap had taken over. For the first time, people — not technology — are the primary constraint on AI progress.
