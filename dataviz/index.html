01 · Adoption Trajectory

From Curiosity
to Core Infrastructure

AI went from an experiment in 38% of enterprises to mission-critical infrastructure in 78% — a journey shaped by a pandemic, a recovery, and one explosive year.

% enterprises reporting AI deployment · 2019–2025
Three Adoption Curves
2019 — Baseline
Cloud AI matures. 58% adopt. Finance and IT lead; adoption concentrated in PoC phase.
2020 — Disruption
COVID halts projects. Drop to 50%. Remote automation accelerates as silver lining.
2021–22 — Rebuild
MLOps maturity. Stable 56% base. Firms move from experimentation to production-grade systems.
2023 ★ — Inflection
GenAI explosion. ChatGPT, GPT-4, Claude. +17pp in 12 months — steepest surge since tracking began.
2025 — Infrastructure
AI agents. Agentic workflows. 78% deployment. ROI realization at enterprise scale.
